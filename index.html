A Court Of Thorn And Roses by Sarah J. Maas

At first I was skeptic about reading this book. I'm a picky reader and many of my friend kept saying it was boring and all that so I kept skipping. But then later I understood just how you can't judge a book by its cover, you can't judge a book by anyone else opinions. If you like it, you like it, that's it. One day, I had no books to read but I really wanted to read one, so I just said let's just try this and god, I regretted not reading it sooner. I loved the plot and literally all the character development of every characters in the book. I loved every character in the book had their own story, rather than being overlooked as side characters. Literally well written. The way Rhysand supported Feyre even when she hated him, that's something I would want in my boyfriend (minus me hating him part but you get me). However I might warn you, the first book was little boring, but after the second book, it gets good. I would really rate it 8/10. If you loved Beauty and Beast, you'll definitely love this one

Shatter Me Series by Taherah Mafi

Ah, my all time favorite book. I was interested because of it's different writing style and also because of Aaron Warner. My insta feed was all about him and I was quite into it so I picked up the book and I couldn't keep it down even for a second. Hell, I ruined my whole sleeping schedule to binge reading all the books in the series. I have read so many book after this series, however, no one could literally top Aaron Warner. He would always be my top 1 fictional boyfriend. The way Juliette transferred from a girl who hated herself and her powers to the girl who learned to love herself and embrace her powers and grew powerful, breaking all the obstacle in her way. It felt a bit personal to me hahah. The way Aaron encouraged her to embrace it rather than being scared for her and that he helped her grow both personally and physically was really so... heart touching. (Ah, man why doesn't guys like Aaron Warner doesn't exist?). Plus, Juliette and Kenji friendship was literally soo wholesome throughout the book. I would totally rate this book 1000/10. If you love reading about found family, Morally grey mc, enemies to lovers, dystopian tropes, then this book is definitely for you.
